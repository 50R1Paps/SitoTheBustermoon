Four years touring around France, Spain and Italy, the debut EP Going To (2018) and the album Mareena Roots gave The Bustermoon the title of “sons of a crazy fusion between the sound of the Zac Brown Band and the attitude of The Beach Boys”. For the new album The Other Pocket, out in Spring 2023, they unleash their folk rock in a new world where they also find ska, ballads and Californian style punk rock. There’s a part of Europe where The Bustermoon have never played yet: 2023 is the year.
